## ðŸŽ¯ NGO Objectives & Mission
The primary objective of Sankalp is social and economic welfare, aiming to provide education to children to foster modern thinking and contribute to the country's economy.

**Key Aims:**
* **Education Advocacy:** Promoting education, specifically for the girl child and women.
* **Food Security:** Feeding hungry people and addressing malnutrition.
* **Socioeconomic Development:** Promoting the use of Information & Communication Technology (ICT) for community development.
* **Child Welfare:** Safeguarding children from abuse, lack of education, and health issues.
